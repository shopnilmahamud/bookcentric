About us

Bookcentric is the first library in Bangladesh that can be accessed from the comfort of one’s home. People in Bangladesh have struggled to find well-stocked and accessible libraries providing original prints of good English books. Bookcentric is here to solve this issue by providing a combination of affordability, variety and convenience that cannot be found anywhere else.
Bookcentric’s treasury holds books for even the most eclectic reader. Here readers will have access to the latest bestsellers, and hundreds of other titles in various genres including Fiction, Young Adult, Fantasy, Thrillers and Mystery, Memoirs, Historical Fiction etc.
One of the biggest challenges one faces in Dhaka is the time-consuming and exhausting ordeal of going from one place to another. Bookcentric not only allows members to choose and order books online but also provides them with delivery as well as pick-up and drop-off points at prime locations in Dhaka and Chittagong.
